## Don't let your leads go cold

Timely follow-up is the difference between a closed sale and a lost one. Cord includes a **Tasks with Due Dates** system integrated directly into the details view of each quote.

Although it's a small feature, it's incredibly powerful to ensure no deal slips through the cracks due to a lack of follow-up.

### How to create a task step-by-step

1. Go to your Dashboard and open the quote you want to track (whether in Draft, Sent, or Viewed status).
2. On the right side panel, look for the **"Tasks and Follow-up"** section.
3. Click on **"+ New Task"**.
4. A small form will open where you must enter:
   - **Description:** For example, *"Call Luis to ask if he has reviewed the quote"*.
   - **Due Date:** Select the exact day on the calendar.
5. Click **Save**.

### Automatic Reminders

Once the task is scheduled, Cord will take care of reminding you.
- In the system, the task will be marked with a color indicator based on its urgency (green if on time, red if overdue).
- When the task date arrives, you will receive an email notification reminding you to make the call or send the message.

Once you complete the follow-up, simply check the `[x]` box next to the task to mark it as done.

> **Pro tip:** We recommend creating a *"Follow-up call"* task immediately after sending the Magic Link, scheduling it for 48 hours after sending.
