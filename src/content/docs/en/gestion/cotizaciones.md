## The Draft Lifecycle

Not all quotes are finished in 5 minutes. Sometimes you need to check prices with suppliers or wait for your manager's approval before sending it.

### 1. Save as Draft
- While creating a quote, you can click **"Save as Draft"** at the bottom right.
- This will securely save all your progress (client, products, discounts).
- The quote will appear on your Kanban board under the "Drafts" column with a gray tag.

### 2. Continue Editing
To resume a draft:
1. Go to the Dashboard.
2. Click on the draft card you want to continue.
3. The details view will open. Click on **"Edit Quote"**.
4. You will be redirected to the quoter exactly where you left off. When finished, you can send it, and its status will change to *Sent*.

## Duplicating Quotes (Time-saver)

If you have a standardized service or want to sell exactly the same thing to a different client, you don't have to start from scratch.

**How to duplicate in the app:**
1. Go to the details view of any quote (regardless of whether it's been paid, canceled, or is a draft).
2. Click the three dots button (•••) in the top right corner.
3. Select **"Duplicate Quote"**.
4. Cord will create an identical new draft. You just need to change the client (if applicable) and adjust prices if necessary.

## Cancellation and Version Control

If you have already sent a quote, but the client asks for a major change, do not edit the same quote (as there could be legal confusion if the client approves while you edit).
- The correct workflow is to **Duplicate** the quote, make the adjustments, and send the new link.
- Then, on the original quote, use the (•••) button and select **"Mark as Canceled"** to keep your pipeline clean.
