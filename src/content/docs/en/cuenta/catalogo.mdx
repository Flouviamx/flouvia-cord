## Manage your SKUs easily

The heart of a B2B quote is product accuracy. Cord allows you to manage a centralized catalog where your sales team simply searches and selects, ensuring the name, SKU, and tax base are always correct.

### 1. Manual Creation

If you have a small catalog or sell services, you can add products one by one from **Catalog > New Product**.
You will be asked for the following data:
- **Product / Service Name**
- **SKU (Internal Code):** Unique identifier.
- **Base Price (Before taxes):** The public list price.
- **Internal Cost (Optional):** If you enter how much the product costs you, Cord will calculate and show you the **Profit Margin** in real-time when building the quote. Your client will never see this cost.
- **Volume Pricing:** Vital in B2B. You can set up automatic rules, for example: *"From 1 to 10 pieces the price is $100, from 11 to 50 pieces the price drops to $85"*. Cord will automatically adjust the price based on the quantity the sales rep adds to the quote.
- **Status (Active / Inactive):** If you discontinue a product or run out of stock long-term, you can mark it as *Inactive* so sales reps can no longer add it to new quotes, without deleting its history.
- **Product/Service and Unit Key (SAT):** Mandatory for CFDI 4.0. We have a built-in smart search engine.
- **Applicable Taxes:** Define if it carries VAT (16%, 8%, 0% or Exempt) or IEPS.

### 2. Bulk Import (Coming Soon)

For B2B companies with hundreds or thousands of references, Cord will soon enable bulk import. 
You will be able to download our **CSV / Excel Template**, paste the information extracted from your ERP (such as SAP, Oracle, or Microsip), and upload it to Cord to update prices, SAT keys, and descriptions in a single move.

<Callout type="info">
**Tip:** Do your prices change constantly?
With the **Cord Public API** (Developers), you can synchronize your catalog in real-time. If you update a price in your ERP, a *webhook* or API call can automatically update it in Cord, ensuring your salespeople always quote with the current price list.
