## Overview

Cord's developer platform allows you to connect your internal systems and automate complex financial workflows through a modern, secure, and event-driven architecture. Our design philosophy focuses on predictable, resource-oriented APIs using standard HTTP responses.

### What can you build?

- **ERP Synchronization:** Keep your product catalog and customer base in sync with your accounting system using the [Products API](/docs/desarrolladores/funciones/productos) and [Clients API](/docs/desarrolladores/funciones/clientes).
- **Event Automation:** Listen to key events (payments, approvals) and trigger real-time actions using [Webhooks](/docs/desarrolladores/herramientas/webhooks). For example, mark an invoice as paid in your system when the client signs the quote in Cord.
- **AI Agents:** Connect smart assistants like Claude or ChatGPT to your financial data using our [MCP Server](/docs/desarrolladores/herramientas/mcp).
- **Embedded Experiences:** Deploy native quoters inside your own customer portal with [Cord Elements](/docs/desarrolladores/herramientas/elements).

### Secure Environments

We build for enterprise development. That's why all Cord accounts include isolated Sandbox environments where you can perform destructive testing (like deleting all test data with a single endpoint) without affecting your live production data. Read more in [Test Environments](/docs/desarrolladores/empezar/pruebas).
