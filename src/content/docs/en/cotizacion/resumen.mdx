## The new B2B standard

Historically, creating a B2B quote involved spreadsheets, exporting static PDFs, sending emails, and endless reply chains to negotiate prices. 

Cord reinvents this workflow by combining a **High-Speed Internal Quoter** (for your sales team) with an **Interactive Web Link** (for your end client), culminating in **Automated CFDI 4.0 Stamping**.

### The Workflow in Cord

To successfully create and close a quote, the process is divided into 5 main stages:

1. **[Client data](/docs/cotizacion/cliente?lang=en):** Select or create your client's commercial and fiscal profile.
2. **[Products and discounts](/docs/cotizacion/productos?lang=en):** Build the proposal by adding SKUs, defining quantities, and applying strategic discounts.
3. **[Advanced options](/docs/cotizacion/opciones?lang=en):** Configure the validity period, currency (MXN, USD), and commercial or legal notes.
4. **[Preview and sending](/docs/cotizacion/envio?lang=en):** Review how the quote will look and share it via a Magic Link.
5. **[Approval and stamping](/docs/cotizacion/timbrado?lang=en):** When the client digitally approves, a single click converts the quote into an official SAT invoice.

<Callout type="info">
**Note:** If you have multiple legal entities configured in your account, remember to select the correct issuing entity before you start quoting.

</Callout>
