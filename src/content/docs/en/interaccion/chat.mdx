## The quote as a workspace

In the traditional B2B model, if a client wants to change a quantity or ask for a different discount, they reply by email, the sales rep edits the Excel file, generates a new PDF (Quote_v2_final.pdf), and sends it back.

In Cord, **the Magic Link is interactive and collaborative.**

### General Chat

At the bottom of the web view, both you and your client will have access to a **Chat Panel**.
- The client can write general questions like: *"Can we adjust the delivery time to 15 days?"*
- The sales rep receives an instant notification and can reply directly on the platform.
- The entire conversation is permanently logged in the history of that quote, giving total visibility to sales managers without having to dig through personal emails.

### Line-Item Comments (Product)

If the quote is very extensive (e.g., 40 different items) and the client has doubts about a specific product, they don't need to write *"Regarding line item 14, the X model server..."*.

Cord allows **line-item level comments**.
1. The client hovers over a specific product in the web quote.
2. Clicks the comment icon 💬 that appears next to the price.
3. Leaves their question: *"Can you swap this model for the 16GB RAM one?"*
4. The sales rep receives the exact alert of which item needs modification.

### Live Update

When the sales rep adjusts a quantity, changes a product, or applies a new discount after the chat negotiation, **the client doesn't need a new link**.

They simply watch the web quote update in real-time in front of their eyes, ready to be approved.
