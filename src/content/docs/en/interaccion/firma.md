## The "OK" via email problem

Traditionally, a sales rep sends a PDF and the client replies "Authorized, proceed". If tomorrow there is a dispute over pricing, terms, or invoice collection, a simple email offers very little legal protection for your company.

Cord solves this by implementing a **Simple Electronic Signature and Audit** layer right at the moment of approval.

## The Secure Approval Process

When your client clicks the **Approve Quote** button inside the Magic Link, the system doesn't approve the document instantly. Before that, it displays a legal signature modal.

To proceed, the client must:
1. **Enter their Full Name.**
2. **Enter their Corporate Email.**
3. **Check a legal box** confirming they have the authority to accept the Terms and Conditions outlined in the quote, as well as the commercial obligation to pay.

### Forensic Data Capture (The Audit Trail)

At the exact moment the client presses "Confirm Signature", Cord invisibly captures a **Technical Audit Trail**:
- **Public IP Address** from which the approval was made.
- **Exact Timestamp** synchronized with secure servers.
- **Cryptographic signature** of the document's state (guaranteeing that prices and terms were not altered by the seller *after* the client signed).

## The Audit Stamp

Once approved, the quote's status changes visually. At the bottom of the document (both in the web view and the subsequently generated downloadable PDF), an **Audit Stamp** is permanently embedded.

This stamp clearly shows:
> *"Digitally approved by [Client Name] ([Client Email]) on [Date and Time] from IP address [192.168.x.x]."*

### Legal Advantages
- **Chargeback Protection:** If the client pays online with a credit card and later attempts a chargeback with their bank claiming they "didn't authorize the purchase," this IP and email record serves as compelling evidence in your favor.
- **Security for Stamping:** Having legal evidence of debt acceptance makes issuing the CFDI 4.0 Invoice a legally sound process, facilitating collections or factoring efforts in case of delays.
