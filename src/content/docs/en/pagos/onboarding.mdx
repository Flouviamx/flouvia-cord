## The Onboarding Process

For money to reach your bank directly without Cord holding it, we use **Stripe Connect**. This means you'll have your own sub-account for payment processing, tied to your bank account, and configured so that Cord charges zero commission—you only pay standard Stripe processing fees.

### Steps to connect your account

1. Go to **Settings > Payments** inside the app.
2. If your status is **"new"**, you will see a welcome screen.
3. Click on the **Start verification** button. This will open a secure embedded Stripe component where you will be asked for information about your company.

### What information is required (KYC)?

Stripe is required by law to verify your identity and the legitimacy of your business (KYC - Know Your Customer). You should have ready:

- **Company Tax ID:** The legal name and tax ID (RFC) you use for billing.
- **Official ID:** Passport or government ID of the legal representative.
- **Bank account (CLABE/IBAN):** The account where you want your sales deposited automatically.
- **Articles of Incorporation:** In some cases (for companies with high sales volume), Stripe might request the articles of incorporation to verify shareholder structure.

<Callout type="warning">
**Important note:** Cord does not store any of this sensitive information. The entire process takes place within a secure Stripe iframe (`<iframe>`), and documents go directly to their PCI Level 1 certified financial servers.
</Callout>

## Account Statuses

Depending on your progress in onboarding, your payment account will show different statuses in the interface:

- **New:** You haven't started the process yet. No automatic payment methods will be available.
- **Pending:** You've started the process but Stripe needs to verify your documents or requires additional uploads. In this state, Card and SPEI methods remain locked.
- **Active:** You're all set! Stripe has verified your identity and your bank account is confirmed. You can now charge, and money will flow automatically.

## Available Payment Methods

Once your status is **Active**, you'll be able to individually toggle the methods you offer on your quotes:

- **Credit and debit card:** Processed instantly (Visa, Mastercard, Amex).
- **Automatic SPEI transfer (MX):** Generates a unique, temporary CLABE for the current quote. When the client transfers the funds, the system automatically marks the payment as completed and notifies you.
- **Manual transfer (Without Stripe):** Displays your traditional bank account details. The client uploads their receipt, and you manually mark the payment as received. This method is available **regardless of the status** of your Stripe account.
