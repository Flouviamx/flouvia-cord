As a regulated institution subject to Anti-Money Laundering (AML) rules, the payment processors integrated into Cord establish transaction processing limits for new accounts.

### Limits per Card Transaction

For recently created accounts (first 30 days):
- **Maximum charge per transaction:** $50,000 MXN.
- **Maximum monthly volume:** $300,000 MXN.

If you send a payment link to a customer for $100,000 MXN and they attempt to pay it with a card during this initial period, the transaction will be declined by our fraud engine.

### Payments via Bank Transfer (SPEI)
Payments received via electronic transfer (SPEI) using the dynamic CLABE accounts that Cord generates per quote **have no limit**. You can collect invoices worth millions of pesos through this channel without friction, since the risk of fraud/chargeback is zero.

### Requesting a Card Limit Increase
If your corporate billing requires processing charges of hundreds of thousands of pesos with corporate cards (B2B):
1. Go to **Settings > Account Verification**.
2. Upload your bank statements from the last 3 months and your Annual Tax Return.
3. Our risk team will permanently raise your transactional processing limit in less than 48 hours.
