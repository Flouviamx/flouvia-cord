Offering financing (Months without Interest) to your corporate clients is an excellent strategy to increase the conversion of high-value proposals.

### Configuration in Stripe

Cord redirects customers to Stripe Checkout for payment. Therefore, the availability of Months without Interest depends on the configuration of your Stripe account in Mexico.

1. Log in to your Stripe account.
2. Go to **Settings > Payment methods > Months without interest**.
3. Select which terms you want to offer (3, 6, 9, 12 months) and for what minimum amounts.

### Customer Experience
Once active in Stripe, when a customer opens the quote in Cord and clicks pay, they will be taken to the Checkout. If the amount exceeds your configured minimum, Stripe will automatically show the terms selector to the customer. 
You will receive your full funds in Stripe's standard timeframe, minus the corresponding bank surcharge that Stripe charges you.
